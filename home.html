NITP Discord Code of Conduct

Note:

We are committed to provide a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all, regardless of level of experience, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or other similar characteristic.

Team NITP Discord

Behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

1) Be kind and courteous to others
  • 2) Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • 3) Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
  • 4) Focusing on what is best for the community
  • 5) Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
  • 6) Showing empathy towards other community members

Unacceptable behavior by participants include:

1) The use of sexualized language or imagery and sexual attention or advances
  • 2) The use of inappropriate images, including in a community member's avatar
  • 3) Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
  • 4) The use of inappropriate language, including in a community member's nickname
  • 5) Any spamming, flaming, baiting or other attention-stealing behavior
  • 6) Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
  • 7) Discussing topics that are overly polarizing, sensitive, or incite arguments. This includes the discussion of polarizing political views, violence, suicide, and rape
  • 8) Responding with “RTFM”, "just google it” or similar phrases in response to help requests
  • 9) Other code of conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

Our Ambition:

The goal of this document is to set the overall tone for our community. This isn’t an exhaustive list of things you can and can't do. Rather, take this document in the spirit in which it’s intended, and try to be your best self.

We value many things beyond technical expertise, including helping and supporting others within our community. Providing a positive experience for other community members can have a much more significant impact than simply providing the correct answer.


Range:

This Code of Conduct applies to all the channels managed by NITP Discord. Violations of this code may affect a person's ability to be within the channels.
The NITP_DISCORD Code of Conduct applies equally to all members of the community, including Admins.
